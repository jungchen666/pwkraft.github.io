Welcome

I am a PhD Candidate in the Department of Political Science at Stony Brook University. My research focuses on the psychological underpinnings of political attitudes, behavior, and reasoning. I am especially interested in the development and application of new statistical methods in the area of political psychology. In my dissertation, I use quantitative text analysis to study how citizens discuss and justify their political preferences and beliefs in open-ended survey responses as well as experiments involving group discussions.

For additional information, please see my CV as well as my research page. You can also find my code on GitHub.
